Cairo

About Cairo

The capital "Cairo" of Egypt, is the largest city of Egypt,with a population of 21.3 million. Cairo is associated with ancient Egypt, as the Giza pyramid complex and the ancient city of Memphis are located in its geographical area. Today, the Egyptian capital has the oldest and largest film and music industries in the Middle East and the Arab world


The Main Airport

Cairo International Airport ( IATA code: CAI) and contains 3 Terminals.


Shopping in Cairo

Shopping in Cairo is very entertaining and interesting!


Shopping Areas and Main markets

Khan el-Khalili is a commercial area located in the heart of the capital and parallel to the Muizz Fatimid street and has the most famous markets specialized in handicrafts and golden jewelry as well as Pharaonic gifts, leather goods, perfumes and many more. Also near to Khan el-Khalili, specifically in the "Moski", you will find many shops for household items, clothes and children's toy stores, but if you are looking for different types of fabrics, you are close enough to "Al-Azhar" area where you will find many fabric shops.

Downtown is famed for the clothing stores for different tastes, as well as shoes and leather goods especially in "Kasr El-Nile" and "Talaat Harb" street, also the two famous areas "Dokki" and "Mohandeseen" can be easily reached from Downtown where you can find lots of shops for the most famous fashion brands along with a number of restaurants that offer fast food or delicious oriental Egyptian food.


Shopping Centers

"Mall of Arabia" located in 6th of October city, contains about 700 shops and includes a huge number of trade centers, telecommunications companies, Cinemas, amusement parks, sports, banks, the dancing fountain and a big food court.

"City Stars" is the first mall in the capital of Cairo and located in Nasr City, consists of 7 levels with many different shops and has a complex of cinemas and hypermarket "Spinneys"

"Cairo Festival City" is the latest mall in Cairo and the biggest, it is situated along the ring road in Cairo, just 15 minutes from Cairo International Airport and 30 minutes from downtown Cairo, it offers many facilities and services and includes many stores. It also has the hypermarket "Carrefour" and the famous home decoration store "IKEA", also "Kidzania" the City of edutainment for children, the dancing fountain and huge green spaces.

Places To Visit In Cairo

Pyramids Of Giza


It is commonly referred to as The Great Pyramids of Giza, Pyramid of Khufu and the Pyramid of Cheops. There are three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex, and the highest one is called the Pyramid of Giza. These pyramids are believed to be constructed in 2580 - 2560 BC which was the 4th Dynasty. The great pyramid of Giza is made up of Limestone and granite and is about 481 feet long.

Zamalek In Cairo


Zamalek is a district in the Cairo, home to parts of the Gezira Island in the Nile River. There are three bridges which connect the island to the river banks, these bridges on west and west sides of the island. There are 19th-century apartments and villas which makes this one of the attractive places to live in all of Egypt. It was also called Jardin des Plantes (which means gardens of plants) under Khedive Ismail.
