Dubai

About Dubai

The city "Dubai" is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai. Established in the 18th century as a small fishing village, the city grew rapidly in the early 21st century into a cosmopolitan metropolis with a focus on tourism and hospitality.

Dubai is one of the world's most popular tourist destinations. It has the second most five-star hotels in the world and the tallest building in the world, the Burj Khalifa.

Dubai Marina, also known as "Marsa Dubai" or "New Dubai", features dazzling skyscrapers, apartment complexes, restaurants, shops and cafes. There are other tourist places for entertainment, that many tourists from all over the world come to visit it like the shopping centers especially in the Global Village, which is the most famous entertaining spot in the region. It provides the guests with a series of stunning events in an open area, as well as the unique taste of shopping and a variety of international cuisines. Dubai Miracle Garden is the most beautiful and largest garden in the world and has more than 45 million different natural flowers with different shapes and odors. It is divided into corridors and arranged in colors and homogeneity to burnish the viewers.

In all parts of Dubai, many hotels are included in the world's greatest hotel chains such as: Burj Al Arab Hotel, Sheraton Jumeirah Beach Resort Dubai, Millennium Plaza Hotel Dubai, Bonnington Jumeirah Lakes Towers Dubai which offer the best services and facilities such as entertainment and fitness centers, as well as luxury restaurants, comfortable and stylish rooms, gardens and other features that provide you with convenience.

Shopping in Dubai

For shopping lovers and those who are interested in fashion, Dubai certainly should be your first choice, as it includes the largest and best shopping centers in the world. When you go shopping in Dubai, you practice a favorite hobby.


Shopping Areas and Main markets

Dubai Mall is the largest mall in the world and one of the most entertaining places in the region for its great capacity. It is one of the best places for shopping, entertainment and tourism where you will find a lot of entertainment stuff popularized worldwide like the Dubai Aquarium.

Mall of the Emirates is a large shopping center in Al Barsha, which contains many halls, cinemas, yard games, various shops and a large theater. The most distinguished thing is the indoor ski slope; it is the first ski slope in the Middle East.

Deira Mall is one of the most famous shopping malls in Dubai. It has a shopping and entertainment center consists of two floors and includes more than 300 shops.

Burjuman Shopping Center is characterized by satisfying the tourists' needs, where you will find many shopping shops for global and local brands. Various local traditional and international restaurants are available as well. Also entertainments places and play area for children.

Dubai Festival City Center meets the needs of Dubai residents, and let the tourists go shopping and enjoy various restaurants, as well as many entertainment places, its modern design, makes it very special as it overlooks the sea through a huge glass interface.


Shopping Centers

The "Gold Souk" also known as "Souk Al Dhab" involves more than 300 shops for gold selling, for this, it is one of the largest gold markets in the world.

"Spice Souk" is one of the oldest retail markets in the old Dubai; it is located in Deira next to the Gold Souk. It includes many of the traditional narrow lanes filled with shops, where the visitors can find what they crave from rare to well-known spices.

Places To Visit In Dubai

La Mer


La Mer is the new beach on the block that has something for everyone including good food, shopping, sand and even a waterpark

Bluewaters


Bluewaters is the proud home of the world's tallest and largest observation wheel, Ain Dubai. The incredible structure stands tall at 250m, surpassing the London Eye, Singapore Flyer and even the High Roller in Las Vegas. Book your tickets today and take in sweeping views of the city’s sparkling coastline. One full rotation aboard lasts nearly 40 minutes, so you’ll have plenty of time to spot Dubai's most famous landmarks, as well as enjoy elevated dining and customisable fun.

clap


A cool, Beirut-born restaurant, there has been heaps of noise about CLAP since it first opened. Housed in the Gate Village Building 11 at Dubai International Financial Centre, and boasting a restaurant, lounge and outdoor terrace, the eatery concentrates on modern Japanese cuisine. There’s a full vegan offering, plus a range of grills from the Josper charcoal oven. Of course, there’s loads of sushi to dig into, and with more than 140 options on the menu, the chic crowd that frequents this hotspot is spoilt for choice. Décor-wise you’ll be greeted by walls lined with books – and a huge chandelier, which was made out of 2,100 toys. It’s something that has to be seen to be believed.
