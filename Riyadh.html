Riyadh

About Riyadh

The capital of Saudi Arabia, "Riyadh" is the largest city on the Arabian Peninsula. Located in the center of the an-Nafud desert, on the eastern part of the Najd plateau, the city sits at an average of 600 meters (2,000 ft) above sea level, and receives around 5 million tourists each year, making it the forty-ninth most visited city in the world and the 6th in the Middle East.

Riyadh is a major financial and administrative center of the nation. You'll find it a busy and very modern place, with much to offer the curious traveler.

The city offers a wide selection of restaurants from cheap to fine dining, including classic Saudi and Middle Eastern cuisine and many Indian and Pakistani options.

The city plays host to several sleek modern shopping malls, full of clothes, electronics, books, CDs, and much more.


The Main Airport

King Khalid Airport (IATA code: RUH) consists of 4 passenger terminals in addition to the Royal Hall.


Shopping in Riyadh

Shopping in the city of Riyadh has a private and pleasure taste. At first you may find that the prices are high, but through negotiations you can get suitable prices.


Shopping Areas and Main markets

Kingdom Centre Tower is one of the most prestigious and luxurious shopping centers in Riyadh, its height is 302.3 m. It includes celebrations hall, libraries, offices, luxury apartments, and hotels. Also a large number of international restaurants, in addition to King Abdullah Mosque which is the second highest mosque in World.

Al Faisaliah Tower is one of the most prominent and famous places in Riyadh at Olaya district and its height reach 266 meters. It consists of shopping centers with major world brands, several luxurious hotels and other buildings.

Hayat Mall is located in King Abdul Aziz Street and contains everything you need from the shops that are filled with world-famous brands, as well as gift shops, and there are many restaurants and cafes to enjoy shopping.


Shopping Centers

Shopping centers represent a large part of Riyadh, and the most important malls are "Carrefour" in Al khaimah markets, which is characterized by the diversity of products like fast foods, fish, clothes and household items which are available at the lowest prices.

The "Khurais Mall" is famous for the outfits for all people from different nationalities, it is located in the Khurais Road, and there is also a park for children for more recreation and entertainment.

"Granada Mall" is one of the best malls in Riyadh at the Imam Abdullah bin Saud bin Abdul Aziz Street. It contains more than 30 restaurants and cafe. For entrance and exit, there are 16 gates and this will facilitate reaching all the directions in the center, and also includes a number of global and local and popular shops.

Places To Visit In Riyadh

Edge of the world


The Edge of the World (its real name is Jebel Fihrayn), one of Saudi’s most popular tourist destinations

King Abdul Aziz Historical Centre


Discovering Saudi Arabia's rich past is an amazing journey that would bring you along thousands of kilometers around the whole Kingdom. An easier way to have an insight of Saudi Arabia's rich History is to visit the King Abdulaziz Historical Center in Riyadh where artifacts from all periods are exhibited and with explanations about their historical significance.

Heet Cave


Saudi Arabia is a country with rarest wild caves. Its caves have great exploration potential with unsurpassed beauty that provides unending curiosity to a caver. Drive to Heet Cave which is about 40km from Riyadh City Center going towards Al Kharj. Heet cave situates at the face of Mount Al Jubayl in Wadi As Sulay in a small village called Heet in Riyadh. Heet cave where you can take best photos,You can enjoy outdoor games, photography and many more. It’s safe place for families as well.
